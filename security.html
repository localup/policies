At LocalUp Solutions, we take the storage and transmission of private data very seriously.  Our security measures include:

  • SSL encryption to protect private data transmitted via the Internet
  • Strong, standards‐based encryption for sensitive data stored in our database
  • A firewall to block unauthorized access to our servers
  • Physical security to prevent unauthorized access to the datacenter
  • Daily backups to guard against data loss
  • The adoption of Payment Card Industry policies & procedures

For detailed information about PCI Data Security Standards, please visit https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/security_standards/pci_dss.shtml.
